Why Join us

Learn about Astronomy and observing techniques. A good way to learn is by doing. Join fellow enthusiasts and take your first steps towards gazing at the wonders above in a safe enviorment.

Benifits of Joining

Enjoy the social aspects of Astro-Club. Our club has members from all walks of life and we all have one thing in common ...Our love of the cosmos. Its a great way to meet new people who share your interests

Expand your knowledge

Learning from people more experienced then you is always benificial here we have Astronomy enthusiasts at all levels of observing . Here in Astro-Club we employ a mentor programme to aide in increasing your knowlege of Astronomy and also in using your equipment to its full potential
