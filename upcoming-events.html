September Meteor Shower

Join us This Thursday night 26/09/2022 at 23:00 to watch the Orionids Meteor Shower. The Orionids are a medium strength shower. In a normal year the Orionids produce 10-20 shower members at maximum. A fun social evening spent watch a fantastic event.
